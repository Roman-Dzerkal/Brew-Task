# ☕ Brew Tasks

> A calm, offline-first desktop task tracker.

## ⚠️ Early development disclaimer

Brew Tasks has only just started and is currently being developed whenever inspiration, time, and energy align.

The app may be incomplete, unstable, confusing, or simply broken in places. Features can change, disappear, or work differently from one version to another.

If you decide to use it, do so entirely at your own risk. Do not rely on Brew Tasks for critical deadlines, important work, or anything you cannot afford to lose.

---

## About

Brew Tasks is a simple desktop task tracker designed for people who want a quieter place to plan their day.

The core idea is straightforward: your tasks should remain available even when the internet is not.

Instead of treating offline mode as an emergency fallback, Brew Tasks is built around the idea that local work should feel natural, immediate, and reliable.

No crowded dashboards. No gamified productivity pressure. No endless notifications.

Just your tasks, your day, and a little more calm.

---

## What it is trying to become

Brew Tasks is intended to help with:

* Capturing tasks before they disappear from your head
* Planning what needs attention today
* Keeping future tasks visible without cluttering the current day
* Organizing work into projects and tags
* Prioritizing what actually matters
* Staying productive when the internet is unavailable
* Creating a calmer and more intentional planning routine

---

## Core principle: offline first 📴

Tasks should not become inaccessible because Wi-Fi disappears.

Brew Tasks is designed around a simple promise:

> Your work should stay with you, even when the internet does not.

Changes are intended to be saved locally first, so the application remains usable during travel, unstable connections, power interruptions, or deliberate time away from the internet.

---

## Planned experience

The application is expected to include spaces such as:

* **Today** — tasks that matter now
* **Inbox** — quick captures and unprocessed ideas
* **Upcoming** — tasks planned for the near future
* **Anytime** — things worth doing without a strict deadline
* **Someday** — ideas that do not need attention yet
* **Projects** — groups of related tasks
* **Tags** — lightweight ways to organize and filter work

Tasks may eventually support details such as:

* Due dates
* Priorities
* Notes
* Checklists
* Projects
* Tags
* Completion history

None of this is guaranteed yet. The project is still finding its shape.

---

## Design direction 🎨

Brew Tasks uses a warm coffee-inspired visual style: soft cream backgrounds, latte surfaces, caramel accents, and dark espresso text.

The goal is not to make productivity feel intense or urgent. It should feel more like opening a notebook beside a cup of coffee.

The primary typeface is **Manrope** — clear, modern, and comfortable for everyday use.
